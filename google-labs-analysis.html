Google's flagship multimodal AI — text, code, reasoning, vision, audio, long-context.

Competitive Advantages

  • Deeply integrated across Google's entire product ecosystem

Science & Research (DeepMind)

Biomolecule AI

Predicts 3D structure of proteins, DNA, RNA, and small molecules. Nobel Prize 2024. Free academic database with 200M+ predicted structures.

Use Cases

  • Vaccine development
  • Enzyme engineering for industrial biotech

Decodes regulatory elements in the genome, connects genetic variants to disease mechanisms. Enables cancer genomics and precision medicine.

Trust & Safety

Watermarking Technology

SynthID

Invisible watermarking in AI-generated images, video, audio, and text. First at-scale deployment of AI content provenance — standardized across all Google generative products.

Strategic Summary

Google's key structural advantage is vertical integration — the same Gemini models power Search, Android, Workspace, YouTube, and all Labs experiments simultaneously. No competitor has that distribution scale. DeepMind's science portfolio (AlphaFold, WeatherNext) is a uniquely defensible moat. Google is catching up in AI agents (Antigravity vs. Cursor, Mariner vs. Operator) and consumer AI assistant experience (Gemini app vs. ChatGPT).
